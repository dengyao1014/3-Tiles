Hidden Achievements and Secret Challenges

Published: March 16, 2024 Author: Achievement Hunter Team

Unlocking Hidden Achievements

Golf Orbit features numerous secret achievements that aren't listed in the main achievements menu. Here's our comprehensive guide to finding and unlocking them all:

Planetary Achievements

1. Mars Explorer

  • Land on all Mars platforms in a single shot
  • Reward: Special Mars-themed ball skin
  • Difficulty: ★★★★☆

2. Space Navigator

  • Complete a shot that passes through all planetary atmospheres
  • Reward: Galaxy trail effect
  • Difficulty: ★★★★★

Special Shot Achievements

1. Perfect Chain Master

Requirements:

  • Achieve a 10x multiplier in a single shot
  • Hit at least 5 different types of surfaces
  • Maintain perfect power timing throughout

Reward: Golden Power Meter

2. Wind Whisperer

Requirements:

  • Score a hole-in-one in maximum wind conditions
  • Don't use any power-ups
  • Perfect power timing required

Reward: Special Wind Effect Trail

Challenge Mode Secrets

Hidden Challenge Levels

Complete these specific actions to unlock secret challenge levels:

  1. Complete all regular levels with 3 stars
  2. Find all hidden coins in World 1
  3. Achieve a specific score combination (hint: look at the numbers in the tutorial)
  4. Play during a full moon (real-world time)

Easter Eggs

  • Input the Konami Code on the main menu for a surprise
  • Click the moon 5 times in the background for a special night mode
  • Find all hidden developer signatures in the levels
  • Complete a level backwards for a special animation

Achievement Hunting Tips

  1. Keep track of your progress with screenshots
  2. Join the Golf Orbit Discord community for hints
  3. Some achievements are only available during special events
  4. Practice specific shots in training mode
  5. Don't forget to check for new achievements with each update
