About Golf Orbit

Golf Orbit is a unique golf simulation game that breaks the boundaries of traditional golf, allowing your golf ball to not only fly into the sky but even reach Mars. In this exciting sports game, you will experience intense one-on-one matches, challenge levels of various difficulties, and pursue perfect hitting skills. By continuously improving your skills, compete for the title of Golf King in this novel and fun golf adventure.

How to play the Golf Orbit Game

The essence of Golf Orbit lies in the pursuit of ultimate driving distance. This simple one-button game is easy to pick up, but achieving a perfect shot requires precise skill. Just hold down the left mouse button to adjust the power, release it to swing and hit the ball. The ball will collide with various objects in the air, with each bounce providing additional flight distance. The system records the distance of each shot, allowing you to see your progress clearly. Your ultimate goal is to surpass your farthest distance record with every swing.

Collect Rewards

Collect coins during the ball‚Äôs flight to upgrade equipment and improve hitting performance. Each shot is an opportunity to break records, and there are various ways to earn points. The farther the hit distance, the more points you receive. Additionally, you can also get extra points through a lucky wheel for unexpected surprises. The perfect strike requires the perfect combination of three key elements: power, speed, and bounce. By earning reward points, you can enhance any one of these three attributes to create your own perfect hitting style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Golf Orbit free?
Yes, Golf Orbit is completely free to play in your web browser. No downloads or purchases are required.
Is Golf Orbit offline?
Golf Orbit can be played online via desktop, mobile, or tablet or downloaded on the App Store for iOS and Google Play for Android.
Is there a way to save my progress in Golf Orbit?
The game automatically saves your progress as you complete levels. As long as you play on the same device and browser, you should be able to continue where you left off.
